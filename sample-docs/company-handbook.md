# NovaTech Solutions - Employee Handbook

## Welcome to NovaTech

Welcome to NovaTech Solutions! We're a Series A fintech startup on a mission to make business payments seamless for small and medium enterprises. This handbook will help you understand our culture, policies, and expectations.

## Our Mission

To simplify business-to-business payments so that every SME can focus on growing, not on chasing invoices. We serve over 2,000 businesses across North America and process $150M in annual payment volume.

## Work Schedule & Remote Policy

Standard working hours are 9:00 AM to 5:30 PM in your local timezone, Monday through Friday. We operate on a hybrid model:

- **In-office days**: Tuesday and Thursday (mandatory)
- **Remote days**: Monday, Wednesday, Friday (optional remote)
- **Core hours**: 10:00 AM to 3:00 PM — all team members must be available during these hours regardless of location
- **Flex hours**: You may shift your start/end times by up to 90 minutes with manager approval

Time off requests should be submitted at least two weeks in advance through BambooHR. Emergency time off should be communicated to your direct manager via Slack as early as possible.

## Benefits Overview

### Health & Wellness
- Medical, dental, and vision insurance (company covers 90% of premiums for employees, 70% for dependents)
- $500/year wellness stipend (gym, mental health apps, ergonomic equipment)
- Free access to Headspace for all employees
- 10 therapy sessions per year covered through our EAP

### Financial
- 401(k) with 4% company match (vested immediately)
- Equity: all full-time employees receive stock options, vesting over 4 years with a 1-year cliff
- Annual performance bonuses of 5-15% based on individual and company performance

### Time Off
- **PTO**: 20 days per year (increases to 25 after 3 years)
- **Sick days**: 10 days per year (no rollover)
- **Parental leave**: 16 weeks paid for primary caregivers, 8 weeks for secondary
- **Company holidays**: 11 days per year (listed in the Holiday Calendar on Notion)
- **Volunteer day**: 1 paid day per year for volunteering

## Engineering Standards

### Code Review Policy
All code changes require at least one approved review before merging. For changes affecting payments processing, two reviewers are required, one of whom must be a senior engineer or tech lead.

### Deployment Process
We deploy to production twice daily (10 AM and 3 PM ET). Deployments are automated through our CI/CD pipeline. Feature flags are required for all customer-facing changes. Rollback procedures are documented in the Engineering Runbook on Confluence.

### On-Call Rotation
Engineering teams participate in a weekly on-call rotation. On-call engineers are expected to respond to P1 incidents within 15 minutes and P2 incidents within 1 hour. Compensation for on-call is an additional $500/week.

## Communication Guidelines

- **Slack** is our primary communication tool. Keep conversations in public channels when possible.
- **Email** is for external communication and formal internal announcements only.
- **Notion** is our knowledge base for documentation, meeting notes, and project specs.
- **Linear** is used for issue tracking and sprint planning.
- Response time expectations: Slack messages within 4 hours during business hours, emails within 24 hours.

## Expense Policy

- Meals during work travel: up to $75/day
- Software subscriptions: requires manager approval for anything over $50/month
- Conference attendance: up to $2,500/year including travel, requires VP approval
- Home office setup: one-time $1,000 stipend for new employees
- All expenses must be submitted through Expensify within 30 days of purchase
